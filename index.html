TimeTrail

Your days, in one private place.

Journal, notes, tasks and expenses — synced to your Google Drive, encrypted so only you can read it.

TimeTrail is a personal record of your days: what you thought, what you did, and what you spent — together in one calm, fast app.

There's no account to create and no company server holding your life. Your data lives on your device, and if you turn on backup it syncs through your own Google Drive, end-to-end encrypted with a passphrase only you know. We couldn't read it if we wanted to.

📓 Journal

Write as many entries per day as you like — a morning note, an evening reflection. Flip back to any date with the calendar strip. Group entries, tag them, find them again in seconds.

📝 Notes

A clean card grid for everything that isn't tied to a date. Drag cards together to form stacks. Pin a note to today's tasks. Link any note to a journal entry, a task, or an expense with a simple @ mention.

✅ Tasks & routines

Simple lists with three honest states — not started, started, done. Set due dates, see them on a built-in calendar, and pin anything to today. Routines handle the repeating stuff: pick the weekdays, and each day tracks its own completion. Your Google Calendar events show up alongside, read-only.

💸 Expenses

A daily ledger that takes five seconds to use: amount, payment type, an optional note. Daily totals and a breakdown by how you paid. Tag expenses, search by amount, description, or date range. USD, INR and EUR supported.

🔍 Search everything

One search box across journal, notes, tasks and expenses — full-text, with filters for date range and tags. Type "10" and find that ₹10 chai.

🔗 Everything connects

Mention a task inside a journal entry. Link an expense to the note about the trip. Tap any link to preview and jump. Your days aren't separate silos — your app shouldn't be either.

☁️ Sync you can trust

  • Your Drive, not our server. Backups live in a hidden app folder of your own Google Drive. We run no server and hold no copy.
  • Encrypted end-to-end. Everything is sealed with AES-256 before it leaves your device, protected by a passphrase only you know.
  • Multi-device that actually merges. Edit the same note on two devices — changes merge, nothing is lost.
  • Works fully offline. Sync is optional. The app is complete without it.

🔒 Private by design

  • No account with us. No ads. No tracking of what you write.
  • Your notes, journals, amounts and tags never leave your device unencrypted.
  • Optional, anonymous crash and usage statistics — never your content.
  • Delete everything, everywhere, any time — one confirmation, gone.

Questions

Do I need an account?

No. Optionally connect your own Google account for backup — that's between you and Google.

What can TimeTrail see?

Nothing you write. Backups are encrypted on your device before upload; only your passphrase unlocks them.

What if I forget my passphrase?

From a device that's still unlocked you can set a new one. If no device is unlocked, encrypted backups are unrecoverable — that's the honest cost of real encryption. Your local data stays usable.

What does it cost?

TimeTrail is free. Backups use your Google Drive's own storage quota.
